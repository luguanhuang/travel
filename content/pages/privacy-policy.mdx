## Introduction

TravelTang provides paid digital travel resources for international visitors traveling to China, including payment setup guides, city travel PDFs, and an AI chatbot for travel questions. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

## Information We Collect

1. **Account Information**
   - We collect details such as your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
   - We use this information to manage your account and deliver purchased services.

2. **Usage Information**
   - We collect information about how you use TravelTang, such as pages visited, features accessed, and chatbot activity.
   - We use this information to improve the product and understand which travel resources are most useful.

3. **Device and Browser Information**
   - We may collect information such as browser type, device type, operating system, and related technical details.
   - We use this information to improve compatibility and reliability.

4. **Payment Information**
   - Payments are processed through third-party providers. We may store billing-related metadata needed to confirm purchases and support order history.
   - We do not describe this as storing full payment card data unless your payment provider actually routes that data through your own system.

5. **Cookies and Similar Technologies**
   - We may use cookies or similar tools to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and improve the product experience.

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- provide access to purchased PDF guides and chatbot features
- process transactions and maintain billing records
- improve the product and customer support experience
- protect the platform from abuse, fraud, or technical misuse
- communicate important account or service updates

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate TravelTang, such as payment, hosting, analytics, authentication, or email tools. We may also disclose information when required by law or when necessary to protect our legal rights or users.

## Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect personal information. No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security, but we aim to reduce risk through standard security practices and controlled access.

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to operate the service, maintain records, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

## Your Choices

You may contact us to request account updates or deletion, subject to legal, operational, or security requirements. You may also manage some browser-level cookie controls directly in your browser settings.

## Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date when appropriate.
